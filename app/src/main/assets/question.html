FAQ

I do not know how to add location for pests?

You can search a specific pest in the search bar of the home page, or tap species icon. enter to the species list. By tap any of species you can jump into the detailed information page and you can find an add location button,which is at the bottom of the page. By tap this button, a pop up window will ask your consent about whether use the current location for pest location or you can add the location manually


Why added pest location unable to display in the map?

Once you fill up the form, your data will be store in the database. Which will take 5-15 minutes to upload. You can refresh the map and see a new marker after 10-30 minutes.


What is harm score, why would I use it?

The harm score represents a predefined harmfulness of each listed species, you can play with it and select a score that you think it is the most appropriate level of harmfulness. Your selection will be recorded into the database and the harm score will updated weekly.


Since some syllables of pests' names are foreign and hard to spell, how do I type them in the searching bar?

In the search page, if you type any character in the searching bar, some pests’ names, states or cities’ names will pop up, which is a helpful prompt tool for you to type.


How do I use this app if I only know what pests look like?

In the species page, our application will display a pest list, which contains all the pest information. You can view the pest list and make a comparison between the pest you meet and the pest photos in the pest list. After choosing, you can get the information you want to know about the specified pest.

How to use the search engine on the homepage?

For the right button, you can click the ‘State’ to choose which state you want to look at. You can input the species’ name or location name For example, we enter the ‘Melbourne’, after searching, it shows some species that exist in Melbourne.


How to know if pests exist in my destination?

To know whether the pests or weeds existing your destinated place, there are two ways to know:

  1. Using the searching bar from the homepage, at the right button, you can select the state of your destination or you can just enter your place at the searching bar. After entering the place, you can see species’ names and images in a list, and you can click them to see more detailed information.
  2. you can just click the ‘Map’ button at the bottom of the application, it will show your current location. If your location entry the range of species, it will notify you. Also, you can choose specific species to see the distribution of each species, you can know whether your destination contains the species.
